Who We Are

Building the Next Generation of Innovators

Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre Ltd (E&I Centre Ltd) is a professional Human Capital Development (HCD) and management consultancy company that provides business and training services for economic empowerment and employment generation — through local enterprise development and business linkages.

We leverage innovation-driven entrepreneurship and strategic partnerships to achieve sustainable economic development and wealth creation across Rivers State and beyond.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence for developing highly competent human capital that is economically self-reliant and employable — through job creation and business linkages.

Mission

Creating sustainable jobs in Nigeria through Local Enterprise Development and Business Linkages.

What We Offer

Our Expertise & Programmes

From vocational training to management consulting, digital skills to HSEQ services — our programmes are designed to develop competent human capital and create pathways to employment and enterprise.

1. Entrepreneurship & Skills Development Training

Our entrepreneurship and vocational skills training is built on our drive to ensure venture creation and the availability of competent human capital for jobs and entrepreneurship pathways.

We equip identified and willing individuals with the mindset, skills, and certification to harness opportunities — including:

  • ESDP (Entrepreneurship & Skills Development Programme) for Tertiary Institutions — integrated into the RSU curriculum as part of the annual Students V-STEP programme.
  • Pre-Retirement Entrepreneurship Training (PRET) — preparing professionals for life after employment.
  • Customer Satisfaction Training (CST)

2. Digital Skills Training & Certification

As a Certiport Authorized Testing Centre, our digital skills programmes begin with a baseline assessment of each learner's current competency level, then provide structured learning, practice testing, and internationally recognized certification.

Courses offered:

  • IC3 Digital Literacy Foundation / Basic IT
  • Google IT Support Professional
  • IBM Data Analytics
  • Cyber Security
  • Autodesk
  • Microsoft Certified Educator
  • QuickBooks
  • Microsoft Office Specialist
  • Entrepreneurship and Small Business (ESB)
  • Communication Skills for Business (CSB)

Programmes span career pathways in Education, IT, Communication, Business Management, Finance, Legal/Admin, Engineering, and Health.

3. Technical & Vocational Skills Training (Construction)

In partnership with strategic stakeholders in the construction industry and Rivers State University, we run a competency-based and demand-driven skills development programme covering:

  • Carpentry / Form Work
  • Plumbing
  • Tiling / Masonry
  • Welding & Fabrication
  • Electrical Wiring / Installation
  • Scaffolding
  • Entrepreneurship & Soft Skills (integrated across all trades)

4. Local Enterprise Development (LED) & Business Linkages

Working with clients and strategic partners, we build the capacity of enterprises in urban and rural areas — equipping them with entrepreneurship competence and access to finance. This serves as a socioeconomic development catalyst to reduce poverty by increasing income through employment generation and economic empowerment.

Our Business Linkage Facilitation programmes focus on:

  • Building SME capacity to competitively meet market opportunities.
  • Increasing connections between skilled individuals and employment/job opportunities.
  • Improving access to finance.

5. Sustainable Development & Management Consulting

E&I Centre Ltd understands the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and facilitates communities, organisations, and individuals to achieve their set objectives and targets.

We also specialize in Management Consultancy — helping organisations improve performance and achieve growth.

Specialist Certifications:

  • Business Continuity Management Systems (PECB)
  • Business Impact Analysis (PECB)
  • Project Management (PECB)
  • Administrative / Secretarial Management
  • Store Keeping / Material Handling
  • Tech-Innovation Business Incubation Management

6. HSEQ Services

In collaboration with our partner company HeRock Integrated Safety Services Ltd, we deliver innovative, cost-effective health, safety, environment, and quality consulting services — particularly to operators in the Nigerian Oil & Gas downstream sector.

Our HSEQ services cover:

  • Corporate-level HSEQ policy setting and risk management.
  • Facility-level HSEQ outsourcing and support.
  • Minimum Industry Safety Training for Downstream Operators.

7. Clarity4D Profiling & Workshop

Our Clarity4D Profiling and Workshop is designed for HR Managers, Heads of Departments, team members, independent trainers, and resource persons — enhancing:

  • Leadership Effectiveness
  • Team Effectiveness
  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Personal Effectiveness
  • Problem Solving
  • Effective Communication
  • Customer Service Delivery
  • Stress & Conflict Management
