Who We Are

Building the Next Generation of Innovators

Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre (E&I Centre Ltd), is a professional Human Capital Development (HCD) and management consultancy company that provides business and training services for economic empowerment and employment generation through local entreprise development and business linkages.

We leverage innovation driven entrepreneurship and strategic partnership to achieve sustainable economic development and wealth creation.

Our Mandate

To create sustainable jobs in Nigeria through Local Enterprise Development and business linkages. We do this by serveing as a centre for entrepreneurial education, innovation support, and capacity development — driving economic growth in Rivers State and across Nigeria.

  • Registered Training Provider with OGTAN (Oil & Gas Trainers Association of Nigeria)
  • Official Consultant for the Tech Innovation Ideas Competition (TIIC)
  • Strategic partner for the Startup Science Tech Ideas Portal
  • Affiliated with Rivers State University for academic enrichment
  • Our core values are deeply rooted in our desire to develop human capital and creating sustainable jobs.

Chika Chinwah

Fellow, Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC)  |  MBA  |  CMC

Chika Chinwah is a seasoned management consultant, entrepreneur, and innovation leader with extensive experience driving organisational transformation and capacity development across Nigeria's oil & gas, technology, and SME sectors.

As the CEO of the Entrepreneurship & Innovation Centre Ltd, he leads a dynamic team committed to unlocking entrepreneurial potential, fostering innovation ecosystems, and delivering impactful training programmes to individuals and corporate organisations alike.

His work bridges the gap between academia, industry, and government — making the E&I Centre a trusted hub for business development, startup incubation, and professional capacity building in Rivers State and beyond.
